## PS COMMAND CHEAT SHEET ##

# Show processes for the current shell/session
example: "$ ps"	

# Show all processes
example "$ ps -e" or "$ ps -A"

# Show processes for a specific user
example: "$ ps -u <user>"

# Show a specific process by PID
"$ ps -p <PID>"

## TIPS ##

Use "$ ps" with "grep" to find specific processes:
example: "$ ps aux | grep nginx"
